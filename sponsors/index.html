Sponsors & partners

The organizations supporting PALNET.

PALNET's educational work is supported by EEG technology vendors, a technology partner, and a coalition of non-profit organizations focused on pediatric anesthesia safety and sustainability.

Vendor partners

PALNET is vendor-agnostic. Both major pediatric processed-EEG vendors contribute educational grants that make our work possible. Their devices are FDA-cleared for pediatric use and display the raw EEG, density spectral array (DSA), and spectral edge frequency (SEF) data our curriculum is built around.

Medtronic
EEG vendor partner

Medtronic

Maker of the BIS Advance anesthesia EEG monitor — FDA-cleared for pediatric use. Medtronic supports PALNET through educational grants that fund curriculum development, site visits, and workshops.

Visit medtronic.com → · Minneapolis, MN, USA

Masimo
EEG vendor partner

Masimo

Maker of the Sedline anesthesia EEG monitor — FDA-cleared for pediatric use. Masimo's educational grants support PALNET's clinician training programs, simulator development, and international workshops.

Visit masimo.com → · Irvine, CA, USA

SmartTiva
Technology partner

SmartTiva

Maker of the SEDSIM portable EEG simulator, which interfaces with both Sedline and BIS Advance to give PALNET workshops a hands-on simulation experience without needing a live patient.

Medellín, Colombia

Collaborating organizations

PALNET works alongside non-profit and academic organizations that share our commitment to pediatric anesthesia safety, neurological outcomes, and environmental sustainability.

Collaborator

Safe Brain Initiative

An international initiative based in Germany focused on neurological outcomes and standardized perioperative care bundles, including EEG-guided anesthesia. PALNET shares teaching faculty and curriculum with SBI.

Collaborator

SmartTots

A public–private partnership between the International Anesthesia Research Society (IARS) and the FDA focused on anesthesia safety for children. PALNET's curriculum supports SmartTots' goal of evidence-based pediatric anesthetic care.

Interested in supporting PALNET?

We welcome conversations with EEG technology companies, foundations, and pediatric anesthesia organizations that share our mission. Sponsorship supports curriculum development, site visits, and the simulator program — and helps us reach institutions that couldn't otherwise afford training.

Site visits

Bring PALNET to your hospital.

A PALNET site visit is two to three days of intensive, in-OR, case-based teaching delivered at your institution — followed by ongoing mentorship to keep your program growing.

1

Before the visit

We work with your team to scope learning goals, identify cases, and prepare the local faculty who will continue teaching after we leave. A pre-visit knowledge assessment establishes a baseline.

2

During the visit

Network teachers spend two to three days in your operating rooms teaching at the bedside during live anesthetics, supplemented by didactic sessions and simulator practice with our purpose-built portable EEG simulator.

3

Train-the-trainer

Every visit invests in local faculty. We work shoulder-to-shoulder with the colleagues who will sustain EEG teaching at your institution, giving them the curriculum, the case library, and the confidence to keep going.

4

After the visit

A post-visit knowledge assessment, ongoing text and video support, and follow-up meetings every two to three months for as long as your team finds them useful.

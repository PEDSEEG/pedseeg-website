About the workshop

PALNET's workshop at ASA brings together pediatric anesthesia clinicians from the United States, Latin America, Asia, and beyond for a half-day of hands-on EEG-guided anesthesia training, offered in two time slots.

Sessions rotate through a video library of annotated pediatric EEG cases and live simulator practice using devices from both major vendors. Network teachers lead small-group instruction with table assignments, and every participant leaves with a PALNET certificate documenting their training.

If you have specific learning goals or want to attend with colleagues from the same institution, mention it in the notes field of the registration form below.

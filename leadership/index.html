The broader teacher network

Beyond the leadership committee, PALNET draws on a roster of approximately ten expert network teachers — pediatric anesthesiologists with deep EEG expertise who deliver site visits and workshops worldwide. International teaching partners contribute curriculum and host workshops in Latin America, Europe, and Asia.

If you are interested in joining our teaching faculty, please get in touch.
